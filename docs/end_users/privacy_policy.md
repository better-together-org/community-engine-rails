# Platform Privacy Policy (End User Summary)

**Purpose:** Explain how the platform handles your personal data and how you can manage it.

## Key Points
- Personal data (email, profile info, activity) is stored to operate the service.
- Invitation-only platforms restrict who can register; public platforms remain opt-in.
- Cookies/trackers (if enabled by the host) require consent and are disclosed in the platform policy.

## Your Controls
- Update profile and visibility settings from **Settings → Privacy** (where available).
- Request data deletion or export through the platform support channel.
- Manage notification and communication preferences from **Settings → Notifications**.

## If You Need Help
- Contact platform support for questions about retention, exports, or consent.
- Review community-specific privacy notices posted by your host platform.
