# Community Management (Platform Organizer Lens)

Platform organizers oversee multiple communities and support community organizers.

## What to Coordinate
- Create or archive communities, ensuring ownership and default roles are set.
- Support community organizers with tooling access and moderation policies.
- Review community health metrics and address abuse trends.
