# Compliance and Legal Guidelines

## Core Responsibilities
- Maintain platform terms of service, privacy policy, and consent records.
- Honor data deletion/export requests and document retention schedules.
- Avoid copying production data into non-production environments.

## Risk Controls
- Audit elevated roles and access to sensitive data.
- Ensure third-party processors are disclosed and have proper agreements.
- Review notification and tracking settings for regional compliance requirements.
